About Me - Personal

When I'm not working, I’m an outdoor-oriented person who enjoys being secluded in nature on a long hike. Over the past few years, I’ve expanded my knowledge by learning how to forage wild fruits and vegetables. Recently, I’ve begun exploring edible mushrooms and the medicinal properties of certain plants and fungi.

As part of my foraging hobby, I’ve been learning to grow mushrooms like Blue Oysters and Lion’s Mane. While growing mushrooms is time-consuming and labor-intensive, I enjoy learning new things and being self-sufficient.

Other hobbies of mine include fishing, crabbing, and riding my motorcycle. My primary fishing has been saltwater, where I love to relax on a pier, watching the sunset with my line in the water. When it comes to riding motorcycles, I love the feeling of freedom it gives me and the sensation of the open road.
